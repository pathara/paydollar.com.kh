About Us

The Company Behind PayDollar Cambodia

PayDollar is an all-in-one digital payment platform developed by AsiaPay. Since its establishment in Hong Kong in 2000, AsiaPay has emerged as a prominent payment service provider in the Asia Pacific region. Our unwavering commitment is to deliver streamlined and swift payment services that empower businesses of all sizes to provide seamless payment processing, enhance sales conversions, optimize operational costs and reduce the risk of fraud. With over two decades of invaluable experience in the payment industry, we possess the expertise to assist businesses in expanding their operations and seamlessly managing their payment processes.

Embracing digitalization with PayDollar future proofs your business, and stay competitive and adaptable in the ever-evolving digital landscape.

We have 17 operational offices across the Asia-Pacific region to cater to your business needs in various countries and regions:

  • Australia (Melbourne and Sydney)
  • Cambodia
  • China (Shanghai and Guangzhou)
  • India
  • Indonesia (Jakarta and Bali)
  • Malaysia
  • Philippines (Manila and Cebu)

  • Taiwan
  • Thailand
  • Vietnam (Hanoi and Ho Chi Minh)

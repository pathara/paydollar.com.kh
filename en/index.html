How PayDollar Benefits Your Business

Multi-payment & Multi-integration

Accept cards and alternative payment methods (APMs) to cover popular payment options. PayDollar offers merchants multiple convenient and feasible integration methods, enabling seamless integration of our payment gateway with their business, including client-post through a browser, API, SDK, and more.

Multi-channel Integration

PayDollar provides multi-channel support to accommodate your business's need such as online, mobile, or retail with consistent payment processing across different channels, allowing for a seamless customer experience and streamlined management of transactions.

Extensive eCommerce Platform Partnerships

We have established partnerships with some of the largest eCommerce platforms such as Shopify, SiteMinder, D-Edge, Magento and more to expand your reach and generate more sales.

Multi-level Reporting

PayDollar offers customizable report generation to cater to your specific requirements, alleviating management efforts and enhancing operational efficiency.

Sales Dashboard & Analytics

PayDollar's Sales Dashboard empowers merchants with powerful insights into sales performance and customer behaviors. Analyze transaction data, customer trends, and purchasing patterns to stay ahead of the competition and drive growth.

Advanced Fraud Protection

Safeguarding your business and customers against fraudulent activities is our utmost priority. PayDollar includes a built-in fraud monitoring tool that promptly notifies you of any suspicious transactions.

Reliabile and Secure

With a proven track record and over 20 years of industry expertise, PayDollar offers merchants a reliable payment processing platform that complies with industry standards and regulations, such as PCI DSS and ISO27001.

Regional Coverage

We have 17 operational offices across the Asia Pacific region. Our local expertise and understanding of regional payment preferences and regulations can benefit businesses that target customers in these areas.

Quality Technical Support

At PayDollar, we offer 24x7 technical support to ensure uninterrupted business operations. Our dedicated support team is always available to address any technical concerns promptly and help you maintain smooth business processes.

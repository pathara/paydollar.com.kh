Frequently Asked Questions

About Our Services

1.1 What are the e-commerce payment acceptance services provided by PayDollar Cambodia?

There are 2 service plans, namely (a) PayDollar Cambodia eCommerce Standard Plan and (b) PayDollar Cambodia eCommerce Premier Plan.

1.2 Who can apply for PayDollar Cambodia e-Commerce payment services?

We accept applications from any businesses, organizations and charitable bodies registered in Cambodia.

1.3 What are the key components of the various PayDollar Cambodia e-Commerce payment services?

We offer an one-stop and total payment acceptance solutions, in which we process multi-lingual, multi-currency and multi-channel payment transactions as well as fund settlements. Apart from the comprehensive ranges of tools on user administration, void and refund, transaction reporting, in addition, we provide a wide range of value-added processing features including Payment Link and Customized Payment Page.

1.4 What are the benefits of using PayDollar Cambodia e-Commerce payment services?

Our e-Commerce payment acceptance service is comprehensive, easy-to-implement, convenient, efficient and economical. Here are just a few benefits for PayDollar Cambodia merchants:

  • Simple and Easy: A simple application (approval given in as soon as 10 days) opens up the door to one-stop payment gateway AND merchant account service. Implementation effort is minimal with no extra hardware or software required.
  • Flexible: Merchants can accept payments through Visa and Mastercard from all customers.
  • Cost-efficient: We offer payment services with competitive set-up costs, monthly fees and transaction commission rates. There are no hidden costs like remittance fee or foreign exchange charge. Rebate is available for high-volume businesses.
  • Partnership Opportunities: Merchants may capitalize the e-commerce ready customer base of PayDollar Cambodia Direct members to grow their businesses.
  • Value-added Services: PayDollar Cambodia's unique features, like Payment Link, Customized Payment Page can be added to standard service to enhance e-business.

1.5 What kind of payment methods can PayDollar Cambodia e-commerce merchants accept?

PayDollar Cambodia merchants may accept world-wide credit card (Visa and Mastercard).

1.6 In what currency does PayDollar Cambodia settle payments received by merchants?

Currently, PayDollar Cambodia e-commerce merchants can accept payments in AUD, BND, CNY, EUR, HKD, IDR, JPY, KRW, MOP, MYR, NZD, PHP, THB, GBP, USD and many more, and range of currencies of Asian countries. Holders of overseas credit cards may also shop at the site, and we settle the transactions in merchant registered currency. The issuing banks of such credit cards convert the payments into their respective currencies and charge the cardholders with such amounts.


1.7 How much do PayDollar Cambodia e-Commerce payment acceptance services cost?

There are only few standard chargeable items for a merchant. There is an one-off set-up charge, a small monthly service charge and transaction commission based on the payments received and a chargeback handling fee, but no other hidden charges. For details, please contact us at .


1.8 Do I have to pay any charges to other parties besides PayDollar Cambodia?

For PayDollar Cambodia e-Commerce plan merchants, there is no addition fee charged by any party other than PayDollar Cambodia. For PayDollar Cambodia Premier plan merchants, the selected bank may levy its charges to the merchant. Please note that there may from time to time be contingent charges (e.g. refund processing charge), which may be collected by PayDollar Cambodia or our partner bank.

However, at the moment you apply for our services, you don't need to have a running web site. You may contact us when you have the site ready for publishing, or you may choose to apply for our services and at the same your site is being developed.

We do not require merchants to install server facilities as long as the information passed from merchants to us does NOT contain sensitive information (in fact, customers have to enter information such as credit card number, only in our payment page). Meanwhile, merchants are suggested to enhance levels of their web sites to provide maximum protection to their customers.


3.2 Does PayDollar Cambodia use encryption for the server and database?

All sensitive information in our database is encrypted and protected behind a firewall system. Besides, at the browser level, is used.


3.3 Does PayDollar Cambodia e-Commerce support the e-commerce software my web site is using?

Basically our payment services are independent of the e-commerce software you are using, as long as it provide the necessary parameters for us to process transactions.For merchant who is using MIVA Merchant or OS Commerce, PayDollar Cambodia provides a related API or scripts for merchant to connect our payment service platform. Merchant can also develop their own API to serve this purpose, and PayDollar Cambodia provides payment module of osCommerce and Zen-Cart to enable merchant integrate with PayDollar Cambodia Payment page from the open source shopping cart.

No. There are many factors that could affect the speed of a web site, like the power of the host server, quantity and size of images in the site, search methods and so on.


3.7 How does my web site feed the information from our shopping cart to your payment page, and vice versa?

When a customer places an order at PayDollar Cambodia merchant's web site and selects the payment method, the customer will be redirected to our payment page for payment processing. All necessary parameters (e.g. transaction amount, order reference number, customer name) will be carried forward to the payment page at the same time. When the payment is processed, the results (either successful or failed) will be shown to the customer in another web page. Such information will also be fed to the designated web page of the merchant. for more sophisticated post-payment processing.

4.5 How does a merchant handle refund to a customer? What are the charges?

The availability of refund service depends on the service plan.

  • PayDollar Cambodia e-Commerce merchants should submit a completed "Refund Requisition Form" to us, stating details of the transaction to be refunded. There is no refund charges and refund charges will be returned to merchants
  • PayDollar Cambodia e-Commerce own-merchant-account customers should raise refund requests directly to our partner bank.

In any case, we monitor refund activity of merchants very closely. We are particularly concerned with excessive refunds since such may mean unsatisfactory service level (like late delivery or stock shortage).


4.6 What is a chargeback?

A chargeback is the forced reversal of a transaction by the credit card issuing bank. Chargebacks take place when we receive notification from our partner bank that the cardholder's card issuing bank has received a dispute notice from the cardholder, whose card had been charged by our merchants. In certain cases defence is possible (e.g. order denial while delivery is completed indeed), given merchants are able to provide adequate evidence. Sometimes (for example, fraudulent card usage) a chargeback is made immediately. Please note that excessive chargebacks may result in service terms revision, service suspension or even termination.


4.7 What is a rolling reserve?

The purpose of which is to prepare and maintain a sum for contingent chargeback requests. The rolling reserve is usually a percentage of monthly transactions (which ranges from 10% to 40%), which will be released to merchant after a short period of time (usually 60 days).


4.8 I'm concerned with the issue of online fraud. What should I do to minimize fraud?
Fraudulent behavior online takes various forms from stolen card to system hacking. As an experienced payment service operator, we would like to suggest the following actions to minimize fraud.
  • Order details
    Merchants should urge customers to provide adequate information upon ordering, especially contact information of the customers. Make sure that the customer is a legitimate cardholder. More attention is required for suspicious orders (like remote delivery addresses or simultaneous multiple orders).
  • Free mail address
    Some fraudsters attempt to mask their identity by using a free mail addresses. While most users of free mail addresses are indeed legitimate, caution should be exercised for orders with free mail addresses, especially when this is the only way to contact the customers.
  • Out-of-norm orders
    Merchants should be wary of orders that falls outside usual ordering patterns, like bulk orders or purchases that greatly exceed the average transaction amount.
  • IP record
    Beware of orders made from odd locations (which are sometimes traceable with the IP addresses), especially where credit card fraud is more common.
  • Proof of delivery
    Get a signed proof of delivery or receipt if available upon retrieval request.
  • Return policy
    Merchants should devise and maintain clear, easy to understand and consistent product return policies to keep customers well informed

An effective way of minimizing losses due to fraudulence is to encourage customers to register as PayDollar Cambodia members. All bank accounts and credit cards of our members are authenticated, meaning that they are virtually "genuine" customers.
